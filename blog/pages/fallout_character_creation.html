Fallout TTRPG Character Creation (some adjustments by Yakasov)

Attributes and Skills

S.P.E.C.I.A.L Attributes

each character in Fallout is defined by seven attributes. a higher ranked attribute represents greater ability.
S.P.E.C.I.A.L attributes can't go below 4 or be increased beyond 10.

when attempting skill checks, these attributes form part of the target number when rolling the dice.

Strength (STR)

strength measures your character's physical prowess.
what it does: used to make melee attacks, increase melee damage, and changes how much you can carry
buy ranks if: you want to be strong and solve problems with brute force

Perception (PER)

your perceptiveness influences how easily you notice danger and how aware you are of your environment.
what it does: affects your initiative order, makes perception checks easier and improves your ability to use Energy Weapons
buy ranks if: you want to be a perceptive person and solve problems by studying your surroundings

Endurance (END)

endurance is your natural toughness and influences how hardy you are overall.
what it does: boosts your health points, improves your ability to shrug off physical dangers in the wasteland, and helps you wield heavy weapons
buy ranks if: you want to be hardy and resilient, and solve problems by pushing through them

Charisma (CHA)

your natural charisma is how much people like you, and how well you can influence them in turn.
what it does: improves your effectiveness at convincing others, helps you barter with people and helps you find safety among others
buy ranks if: you want to be diplomatic and persuasive, or you wish to solve problems by talking your way out of them

your intelligence measures your smarts and how well you can apply yourself in the wasteland.
what it does: improves your ability to assess obstacles and hazards, and helps you remember and know things by studying the world around you
buy ranks if: you want to be cunning and clever, and find the most effective solution to your problems

Agility (AGL)

agility measures how fit and agile you are, and helps keep you quick and lean.
what it does: helps you to balance and control your body more precisely, and improves your ability to make ranged attacks with Small Guns
buy ranks if: you want to be nimble, and want to solve problems with accuracy and skill

Luck (LCK)

luck describes how much fortune smiles down on you, turning fickle forces in your favour.
what it does: helps you come out on top when you should be at the bottom, and could influence chances related to any event
buy ranks if: you want to add story details, improve skill check results, or re-roll dice
